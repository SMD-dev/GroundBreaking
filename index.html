  • Bio

    Groundbreaking is a one man alternative and electronic project, focusing on heavy rhythms, loud synths and in-your-face lyrics.
    Shaun Harper began writing, producing and recording his own material under 'Groundbreaking' in 2012. Since then he has self released 4 full length original albums. Groundbreaking draws influence from a number of diverse artists such as Nine Inch Nails and Linkin Park.
